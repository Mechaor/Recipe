Grandma's Lemon Meringue Pie

Grandma's Lemon Meringue Pie

One 9-inch pie

30 min - Prep time

10 min - Cook time

40 min - Total

8 Servings

INGREDIENTS

  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 2 tbsp all-purpose flour
  • 3 tbsp cornstarch
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1 1/2 cups water
  • 2 lemons, juiced and zested
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 4 egg yolks, beaten
  • 1 (9 inch) pie crust, baked
  • 4 egg whites
  • 6 tbsp white sugar

DIRECTIONS

  1. Preheat oven:Preheat oven to 360 degrees F(175 degrees C).
  2. Make Lemon Fillings:In a medium Saucepan...
    • Whisk together 1 cup sugar, flour, cornstarch, and salt.
    • Stir in water, lemon juice and lemon zested.
    • Cook over medium-high heat, stiring frequently, until mixture comes to boil.
    • Stir in butter.
    • Place egg yolk in a small bowl and gradually whisk in 1/2 cup of hot sugar mixture.
    • Whisk egg yolk mixture back into the remaining sugar mixture.
    • Bring to a boil and continue to cook while stiring constantly until thick.
    • Remove from heat.
    • Pour filling into baked pastry Shel.
  3. Make Meringue:In a large glass of metal bowl...
    • Whip egg till foamy
    • Add sugar gradually, and continue to whip until stiff peaks foam.
    • Spread Meringue over pie, sealing the edge at the crust.
  4. Bake:Bake in a preheated oven for 10 minutes or until meringue is golden brown.
